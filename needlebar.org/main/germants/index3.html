German-made Transverse Shuttle Machines

Identification Guide

Part Three - Low Arm, Fiddle-Based Machines

This low-arm and fiddle-shaped bed configuration is basically similar to the Singer Model 12 New Family machine, which was extremely popular. Many German makers made their own versions.

This page is far from complete, so you will see further machines and information added from time to time.
