GERMAN SEWING MACHINE ATTACHMENT TINS

INTRODUCTION

These fine and very interesting Attachment Tins are mostly from a collection brought together by a NeedleBar member.

German sewing machine attachment tins were often very striking and decorative, sometimes even including mirrors on the inside!
