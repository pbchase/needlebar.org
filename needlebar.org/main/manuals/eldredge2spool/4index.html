This machine is supplied with certificate of warranty, properly dated and signed, good for ten years from date and covering the breakage of parts that prove defective in any way. The warranty does not include attachments, needles or spool cases.

Remember that every machine before leaving the factory is thoroughly tested on all kinds of work and that it must be right when received. Should there be anything about the machine which you do not understand, correspond or communicate with the manufacturer or your dealer before condemning it.

In ordering needles, state they are for Eldredge Two Spool Machine and give size wanted. If you cannot procure genuine needles marked Eldredge Two Spool you can use regular Singer V.S. No. 2 needles.

Always speak a good word for your machine whenever and wherever you can.
