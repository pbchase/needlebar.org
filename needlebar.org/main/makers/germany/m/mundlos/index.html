Mundlos AG, Magdeburg, Germany

The Mundlos company was located in Magdeburg in the North of Germany, halfway between Berlin and Hannover.

They produced sewing machines between 1863 and 1961 and many of their machines bear the name "Original Victoria". Other machine names used include Dupla and Wilhelmina.

Some toy machines which bear the name Mundlos were actually made by the toy machine maker F. W. Müller in Berlin.
