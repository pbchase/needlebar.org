Grace Brothers

Sydney, NSW

(Information courtesy of Chris Clark and Grace Brother's web site)

Albert and Joseph Grace opened their store in 1895, they developed the first suburban store and first shopping centre. Renowned for honest trading and good service.

Joined with Myer in 1983/9 to become the largest store in Australia, but still trades as Grace Brothers.
